Digital Health Intelligence tool

This tool will help you find and package public health data and evidence. It lets you collect data from Fingertips and other sources into a shared workspace that people you choose can view and contribute to.

You and your team can add tags, notes and context to the data you find.

You’ll also be able to see what other teams working with the same data have tagged or found useful. This will help you share knowledge, build on existing best practice and save time when packaging information.
